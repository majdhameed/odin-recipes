Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian pasta dish made with wide,
flat pasta sheets layered with sauces (like meat or tomato),
cheeses (such as ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan), and other ingredients,
then baked until bubbly and golden

ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Canned Tomatoes
  • Fresh herbs
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Eggs

Directions

  1. Cook the meat
  2. Cook the Noodles
  3. Make the Cheese layer
  4. Assemble the lasagna
  5. bake the lasagna
